#The reserving function for the Search Function:

ex) The engineer Kyle Moylan, is looking for how the Matt Foundation waterproofing needs to be installed during the deep excavation. He want to check what he needs to look for and see other submittal example that other engineers handle.

2. He type Mat Foundation, Waterpooofing in the Search bar.

3. The search bar populate the Submittal that is approved(with correction as noted) that another engineer ( Mariam). If he have a further question, he can connect with Mariam to ask further question through email or phone.
