Project Work Sequence Summary - 06/01/2022

  • Any joint, dual, or phased occupancy requirement – None
  • Only MEP Subcontractor will be required to do more than one mobilization.
  • Work sequence narrative for self-performed crafts/trades and subcontractor trades are included.

Work Item 1: Early site construction: Demolition, Excavation, New entryway, sanitary sewer, and Storm drain.

‍

First, the construction team starts the demolition and early site excavation. This will offer a clear foundation for all other construction operations. The next step of the work Sequence is the Storm Drain and Sanitary Sewer. These become the First item because the Storm drains are required to be finished by the civil drawings before the building structure can begin. the construction work sequence of this package will be:

‍

  • Demolition -> Excavation -> excavation zone 2 > Storm Drain -> Sanitary Sewer -> pavement of new entryway and parking -> connecting to existing street.
  • subcontractor mobilization: excavation subcontractor/MEP contractor for early utilities
  • ‍

After the Storm Drain, the Sanitary Sewer will be completed. Even though Sanitary Sewer is not required to be finished by the Civil Engineer, the project team decided that finishing a Sanitary sewer will provide a logical flow of work sequence. It is because the sanitary sewer is located at the ground level below the building structures.

After we completed the Storm drain and Sanitary sewer, we will move to the parking lot and driveway construction. Usually, these construction sequences come after building construction, but our project team decided to construct the entryway and parking first because of the following reason. 

‍

  1. The project team will construct new parking and entryway. this will ease and help the site logistics for all subcontractors and trade partners.
  2. The Jobsite provides enough room; therefore construction of the entryway will not provide any building construction operation.
  3. The excavation will offer all the foundational excavation for both early MEP excavation and building foundation excavation early on.

‍

Work Item 2: Substructure and Post tension Superstructure

‍

After the Storm drain and early site works are finished, the Project team will pursue substructure and post-tension superstructure. We grouped these activities based on the following reason: 1) both structures are concrete structures. 2) Both structure systems are divided into two zones. The building Utilities will be installed before the building structure. After the building Utilities, Zone 1 construction will follow. The activity Item of the zone 1 activities are:

‍

  • Pile Cap -> Grade beam -> Structural Footing -> Structural Wall -> Structural Columns -> Slab on Grade
  • There is a requirement for the environmental retaining wall, for noise control. this retaining wall will be constructed as the first construction after footing.
  • ‍
  • The team decided to pursue footing and wall first because footings and walls are poured together per structural drawings.
  • The construction zone is divided as indicated in the drawings.

‍

  • The level 2 PT deck is also divided into two zones, because of the Pour Strip requirements.

Work Item 3: Level 3 and Roof Superstructure

‍

After finishing the concrete construction, the Project team will pursue Superstructure. The superstructure system is mainly composed of the wood framing structure. Because of this reason, the team did not divide the work zone. The team will start the construction process from the West side of the project and move toward to East. The construction sequences of the level 3 and roof superstructures are:

  • -> install floor joist -> install floor sheathing -> install wall stud and framing -> install wall sheathing.
  • Level 3 - level 4 -> level 5 -> roof
  • The roof structure will be similar but includes waterproofing and a mechanical room as an additional work item.
  • Subcontractor mobilization: elevator subcontractor. subcontractor in operation: MEP contractor, furnishing contractor, exterior and drywall subcontractor will mobilize during this phase to prepare interior rough-in, furnishing, and exterior installation.

Work Item 4: Core: Electrical room, Elevator, and Stair

The elevator, Electrical room, and stairs require expedited operation because they offer permanent power and access to the different floors. The electrical room is located on level 1. Therefore, as soon as the level 2 PT deck is dried and completed, electrical room construction will follow. The stair construction will follow the structural construction so that it can offer access to each floor. As soon as the level 5 structure is completed, the elevator will be installed.

  • Subcontractor mobilization: elevator subcontractor. subcontractor in operation: MEP contractor, furnishing contractor, exterior and drywall subcontractor

 Work Item 5: Interiors, Furnishes, and exterior Cladding

After the structure is substantial, the interior construction and exterior cladding will be following and chase the structure construction. The interior and exterior construction will happen simultaneously because their schedules are relying on the structure and do not share the same work zone. The work sequence of the interior construction is following.

  • Install Wall Framing -> Install MEPF Rough in -> Install GWB ceiling -> interior Work -> MEPF Trim out -> Furnishes and Finishes.

‍

Work Item 6: Final Project Closeout

The last work item would be the Final Project Closeout. During this work phase, the construction project will be focused on the punch list, startup, commissioning, and inspection. The project team will assist the owner and Architect to resolve any issues. The Final project closeout work sequence will be following:

  • Punchlist -> Startup -> Commission -> Punchlist cleanup -> Final Clean -> Final city Inspection -> Completion

‍

Summary

‍

In this work sequence narrative, the project team described the process of the storm drain, Substructure, superstructure, interior and exterior, Exterior Improvement, and Final Project closeout. The Substructure and PT deck will be divided into zone. The Superstructure will start from the West to the East.
